Artificial Intelligence
Artificial intelligence has two types:
Weak artificial intelligence It is a weak system that does a one particular job. It includes video games like chess from above and Siri, Alexa and other personal assistants or from Google search , IBM's Watson and Image recognition software
strong artificial intelligence It is a system that performs the tasks that a person does and this system may be more complex. There are these types in hospital operating rooms and self-driving cars.
